Bihar: Land of Rich Cultural Heritage

The World Famous Nalanda University is located in the town of Rajgir in Nalanda district.It is one of the oldest universities in the world and was known as a storehouse of Knowledge during ancient days

It attracts students from different parts of the world including Iran,Japan,Greece,China and Korea

Bihar Connectivity

By Road

There is a vast network of National Highways and State Highways which connects Bihar with major regions of the country

By Rail

An excellent network of railway connects major and minor regions of Bihar to different parts of the country through rail. Patna, Gaya and Bhagalpur are main railheads of Bihar.

By Air

Jayprakash Narayan Airport (Patna) and Bodhgaya Airport (Gaya) are the two important airports of Bihar.
